Let us prepare Chocolate Ice Cream

  1. Chocolate
  2. Milk
  3. Sugar
  4. Vanilla Essence
  5. Ice Cubes
  6. Butter
  7. Condensed Milk
  8. Eggs
  9. Cocoa Powder
  10. Nuts

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the chocolate, milk, and sugar.
  2. Add the vanilla essence and ice cubes, and blend until smooth.
  3. In a separate pan, melt the butter and add the condensed milk.
  4. Whisk in the eggs, cocoa powder, and nuts until well combined.
  5. Pour the chocolate mixture into the pan and freeze for 4-5 hours.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy your homemade chocolate ice cream!
