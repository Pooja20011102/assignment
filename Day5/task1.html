A remarkable feature of this century is the advancement of science and its impact on nearly every aspect of society. Today's scientists are exploring broader areas of knowledge, building on the groundwork established by earlier generations. Modern civilization heavily relies on scientists and inventors. We depend on doctors investigating the causes and cures of diseases, chemists analyzing our food and ensuring clean water, entomologists combating insect pests, engineers who transcend barriers of time and space, and many other specialists who contribute to agricultural and industrial development. The progress in science and technology has connected different parts of the world more closely than ever before, enabling us to reach far-off places in a very short time.
