grid

Introduces the character and their world (status quo)
The inciting incident occurs, disrupting the status quo, leading to a "lock-in" where the character is committed to a goal or journey
The protagonist sets out to achieve their goal and encounters initial obstacles.
The protagonist faces larger, more difficult obstacles, leading to a first failure to achieve the goal
The character is now further from their goal and a "campfire" scene occurs where backstory is revealed
A "last push" to achieve the initial goal is successful, but it creates or exacerbates a new, unresolved conflict.
The protagonist addresses the new tension from the previous sequence.
The final conflict is resolved, and a new status quo is established, often showing the character's need has been met.
